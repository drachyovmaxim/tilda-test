Questions

1
What are all the different kinds of dance classes you offer? Can anyone take them?
You will start as a beginner, or more experienced student, learning and perfecting moves to the most popular tunes. You can find a description of each class on the Classes Page.
2
How do I choose a class that is right for me?
If you are a beginner, please look for classes that are "Good for beginners" or for "All levels welcome." Find the descriptions on the Timetable page.
3
How long are the classes?
The classes are 90 minutes each, with a 10 minute break in the middle.
4
Do you have Zumba Classes?
Not yet, but we have plans to extend the program.
5
What should I wear?
Wear anything that you feel good moving around in. Also, sneakers or "dance sneakers" are the best types of shoes to wear.
6
Do you offer workshops with acclaimed dancers?
Yes! Several times a year. They will be posted on our website and you can join our mailing list to be notified.
